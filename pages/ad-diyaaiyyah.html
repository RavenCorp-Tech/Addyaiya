Mission & initiatives

Dār al-Ḥadīth aḍ-Ḍiyāʾiyyah oversees the translation, publication, and dissemination of the Shaykh's research, lectures, and fatāwá. It also facilitates mentorship programs and scholarly review circles.

Current initiatives include establishing a digital manuscript library, hosting live research seminars, and supporting students through structured curricula and guided readings.
